About Tessara

Closing the gap between wanting to help and actually helping

Tessara was started by a local resident who believes most people want to help their community ‚Äî but the gap between good intentions and actually showing up is too wide.

Tessara exists to close that gap by making it simple to find clearly described volunteer opportunities right here in the Michiana area. It's free, it's local, and it's focused on making real connections between neighbors who want to help and organizations that need them.

This is an early version and we're building it based on community feedback. If you have ideas, questions, or want to get involved, we'd love to hear from you.
