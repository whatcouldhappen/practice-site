Tessara

Turning good intentions into work.

A simple, local way to match volunteers with nonprofits — without making it complicated.

Welcome

Tessara helps people who want to help—find a clear next step. Whether you’re an individual looking for a good fit, or a nonprofit trying to fill real needs, we make it easier to connect and follow through.

This site is an early version. We’re building it in public, learning as we go, and improving it based on feedback.

For Volunteers

If you want to volunteer but don’t want a maze of forms or uncertainty, you’re in the right place. The goal is to match you with opportunities that fit your availability, interests, and comfort level.

  • Browse opportunities that are clearly described (time, location, expectations).
  • Start small if you want—one-time or short-term options are welcome.
  • Track what you did and what you learned, so progress doesn’t disappear.

For Nonprofits

If you’re a nonprofit, we can help you turn “we need volunteers” into specific roles people can actually say yes to. Clear roles reduce drop-off and make volunteers more successful.

  • Post roles with concrete duties, schedule, and point of contact.
  • Receive volunteers who are a better fit (availability + interest).
  • Close the loop: confirm start dates and outcomes so everyone learns what works.
