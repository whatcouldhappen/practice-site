This page will eventually let you submit volunteer roles in a consistent format and receive better-matched volunteers. For now, it explains what we’re building and what information helps roles succeed.

What you’ll be able to do

  • Post volunteer roles with clear duties, schedule, and a point of contact.
  • Indicate requirements (background check, lifting, training, etc.) without guesswork.
  • Confirm starts and outcomes so we learn what actually works.

What makes a role “say-yes-able”

  • A specific task list (not “general help”).
  • Time expectations (one-time vs recurring; 2 hrs/week vs 10 hrs/month).
  • Where to show up and who to contact.
  • What success looks like after the first day/week.

Clarity reduces drop-off and makes volunteers more effective.
