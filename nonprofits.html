For Nonprofits

Better volunteers start with better listings

The problem

You need volunteers. But posting on Facebook and hoping for the best gets you people who don't show up, or who arrive not knowing what they signed up for. You end up spending more time managing confusion than getting work done.

What's different about Tessara

Every listing on Tessara requires clear expectations — what's needed, where, how long, and what skills help. That means when someone says "I'm interested," they actually know what they're getting into. You get volunteers who show up prepared.

How it works

You fill out a short form below. We review the listing to make sure it's clear and complete, then it goes live on the site. Interested volunteers see your listing and contact you directly. No accounts to manage, no software to learn.

Free to use

No fees, no tiers, no catches. Tessara is completely free for all local nonprofits.

Clear expectations

Structured listings mean volunteers arrive knowing exactly what to expect.

Local focus

Built specifically for the Michiana area. Your neighbors helping your organization.

Simple process

Fill out the form, we review it, it goes live. That's the whole process.
