This page will eventually let you create a simple profile, browse opportunities, and track what you’ve done. For now, it explains what we’re building and what information we’ll ask for.

What you’ll be able to do

  • Find opportunities that match your availability and interests.
  • Start small: one-time, short-term, or “try it once” roles.
  • Track hours and accomplishments so progress doesn’t vanish.

What we’ll ask (later)

  • Availability (days/times you’re open)
  • Interests (kids, food pantry, tutoring, construction, admin, etc.)
  • Preferred distance / location
  • Comfort level (public-facing, behind-the-scenes, physical activity, etc.)

We’ll keep this lightweight. The goal is fewer forms, not more.
